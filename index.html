Get away at a quaint little cottage, just outside of Caseville.
​
This 2 bedroom, 1 bath is nice get away for the summer! The cottage has 4 beds total, not including two large and comfortable couches. The house comfortable sleeps seven. There is room for an air mattress and sleeping bags.
The living room has a TV and soft seating, a full kitchen and a bathroom with shower.

In the backyard, you will find a fire pit, outdoor grill, and a picnic table and chairs. You are welcome to enjoy the space outback, make this place your home!

Just a short walk from a public beach “Philips Beach”, where you can enjoy a swim in Lake Huron. There is so much to see and do in the local area. From swimming, fishing, goofy golfing, kayaking, to shopping, and exploring Port Austin or Caseville, this place has it all for a relaxing time.

Fun in the Summer, lovely in the Spring, quiet in the Winter….
